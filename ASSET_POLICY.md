# Asset Policy

[简体中文](./ASSET_POLICY.zh-CN.md)

Codex Pet Lab publishes only assets that can be shared responsibly in a public repository.

## Allowed public assets

- Original characters created for this project
- Contributor-owned artwork submitted by its rights holder
- AI-assisted original artwork that does not intentionally imitate a real person, protected character, signature costume, logo, or living artist's distinctive style
- Third-party material with a compatible redistribution license and complete attribution

## Not accepted

- Celebrity or other real-person likenesses without documented permission
- Copyrighted animation, game, comic, film, or television characters
- Traced, recolored, or lightly altered third-party artwork
- Team marks, product logos, watermarks, or branding without permission
- Assets whose origin or redistribution rights cannot be explained

## Local imports

The browser studio may import a user's private local pet package. Local import does not grant redistribution rights, does not upload the package, and does not make that asset eligible for this repository.

## Contribution evidence

Every new public pet must include a short provenance note covering who directed or created it, whether generative tools were used, what references were used, and why it is safe to redistribute. Maintainers may request source files or remove an asset when credible rights concerns arise.

Project assets are distributed under the repository license only to the extent the contributor or project has rights to license them. This policy is a contribution rule, not legal advice.
